More About Me

Bontle's background

I was born, bred and buttered in Cape Town.
Bontle is a pastor's kid, my parents are Xolile and Vuyelwa Nxoyi.
I am the second born of 4 children. So, cutting to the chase, I started singing at quite a tender age. At 11 I was in the church teenage choir,
Yes I wasn't a teenager at the time but when Jesus says yes, aint nobody can say no, so I grew into it and fell inlove with it, got passionate and at 16 my dad put me in the worship team,
there my creative juices started flowing and I realized I could write and compose songs which we would sing at church. At the time I was doing Physics and Maths, because I was an aspiring doctor.
I later decided that music has become life!! Cutting the long story short, amongst the many things I do and aspire to do,
music is still my first love.
